OTC-only scope

USDT.z (ticker: USDTz) is intended for OTC settlement and collateral use cases.

No official public DEX trading or market-making is provided. A minimal PancakeSwap v3 pool exists only for price discovery; no liquidity commitments, no endorsements of secondary markets.

Not affiliated with Tether or USDtez (USDTZ). On-chain references prevail over any text materials.

OTC & Collateral Policy

USDTz is not offered for public exchange trading. All transfers and settlements are handled via OTC channels or as collateral arrangements with counterparties. Any third-party liquidity pools or secondary markets are not official nor supported. Not affiliated with Tether or USDtez (USDTZ).

Security & Control Status

The USDTz contract has permanently disabled any form of privileged access:

  • ✅ Ownership Renounced: owner() = 0x000...0000 → No admin wallet exists
  • ✅ Pauser Deactivated: currentPauser() = 0x000...0001 → pause()/unpause() disabled
  • ✅ Fixed Supply: No mint/burn functions beyond constructor
  • ✅ No blacklist / tax / trading restriction logic

All values are verifiable on-chain. These changes ensure full immutability and trustless operation.
